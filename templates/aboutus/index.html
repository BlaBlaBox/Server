About Us

HISTORY
In 9.17.2018 bla bla box's life adventure started with an lecture assignment. Four friends who are enthuastic about foreign movies and television shows, decided to form an application depending on their passions. The name "bla bla box" was inspired from these four students' lecture teacher, who pronounces the term "bla" in a
very sweet way.

MISSION
The movie industry is growing swiftly. People's movie watching preferences are shifting to the online movie platforms in modern era. The investments that made on the online stream projects are increasing. The aim of this application is to form an e-commetce website that allows people to buy or rent movies.

VISION
We will work hard everyday to make people to be able to watch movies and increase their cultural status by offerin cheap, stable and faster performance.
